Some common questions
were often asked

What is your design style?

This question helps understand the designer's aesthetic preferences and whether they align with the client's vision.

What is the timeline for the project?

Clients often inquire about the expected duration of the project to plan accordingly and manage expectations.

What is the budget for the project?

Knowing the budget allows the designer to propose suitable options and ensure that the project stays within the client's financial constraints.

Can I see examples of your previous work?

Clients often request to see the designer's portfolio to gauge their expertise, style, and quality of work.

Do you provide full-service design or consultation-only services?

Some clients may require comprehensive design services, while others may only need guidance and advice. Clarifying this upfront helps set expectations.

How involved will I be in the decision-making process?

Clients often want to know their level of involvement in the design process, including material selection, furniture choices, and overall design direction.
